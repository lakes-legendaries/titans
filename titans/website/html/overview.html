Titans Of Eden is a fast-paced deck-building card game for 2-6 players. We created Titans because we love deck-building games, but we often get frustrated by how slow and unbalanced other games can feel, and how limited their strategies tend to be. Titans Of Eden stands out because of its:



Interactive Gameplay:

Virtually every card has one or more abilities, and most abilities are designed to interact with your opponents. Additionally, just about all the cards you can add to your deck belong to one of the four main elements, and the strategies and abilities of each element are designed to counteract one other element.





Balanced Set-Up System:

Titans Of Eden uses the Empire Setup System, which ensures that every game contains multiple balanced strategies and counter-strategies. This make it so that your opponent has to engage with you: They can't just ignore what you're doing to focus on their own strategy.



Rapid-Fire Gameplay:

Titans Of Eden uses the No-Wait Play System, which has all players playing cards every single turn. Unlike other deck-building games, you don't have to spend ~75% of your time just waiting for your opponents to go.



Depth Of Strategy:

Titans Of Eden is a very strategic game. After you master the base game, check out the Constructed Format, which lets you build your own spellbook before playing.
